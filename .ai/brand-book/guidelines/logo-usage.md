# Logo Usage

## Logo Variants

| Variant | File | Use case |
|---------|------|----------|
| Full logo (dark bg) | `logos/svg/logo-light.svg` | On dark backgrounds (default) |
| Full logo (light bg) | `logos/svg/logo-dark.svg` | On light/white backgrounds |
| Logomark (dark bg) | `logos/svg/logomark.svg` | Favicon, app icon, small contexts |
| Logomark (light bg) | `logos/svg/logomark-light.svg` | App icon on light backgrounds |


## Logo Construction

The logo is a two-part wordmark:
- **"JT"** — Playfair Display Bold, accent color (#F59E0B)
- **"Digital"** — Inter Medium, secondary text color (slate-300 on dark, slate-800 on light)

The gap between "JT" and "Digital" uses Tailwind `gap-3` (0.75rem / 12px).

## Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space of 1x the height of the "JT" text around the logo on all sides.

## Minimum Size

- Full logo: minimum width 120px
- Logomark: minimum width 24px

## Don'ts

- Don't change the colors of the logo components
- Don't stretch or distort the logo
- Don't add effects (shadows, outlines, gradients) to the logo
- Don't place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- Don't rearrange the logo components (JT must always precede Digital)
